## Get Started draft

Get Started

Need: paid Cloudflare account (5$)

Go to Cloudflare.com
create account
on the left, go to Build > Workers plans > Purchase Workers Paid
enable storage, Build > Storage & databases > R2 Object Storage > Overview > Get free subscription
Go to deploy.gsv.space, sign in and follow the instructions
Congratulations on your very own GSV!
